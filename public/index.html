Proven Learning Methods

The Leitner System

Physical flashcard method that moves mastered words into less frequent review piles. Vocai digitizes this with 5 boxes: Daily → 3 days → Weekly → Bi-weekly → Monthly.

Contextual Learning

Learn words within phrases and full sentences to understand usage, not isolated definitions. Every flashcard includes real-world example sentences.

Vocabulary Island Technique

Focus on one topic at a time to see the same vocabulary reappear in different contexts. Build thematic word collections naturally.

Mnemonics & Visual Memory

AI generates image prompts and mental associations to connect new words to familiar concepts. Visual learning boosts retention by 65%.
